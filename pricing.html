Common Questions

Pricing Questions, Answered Clearly.

Here are answers to common questions about quotations, payments, revisions, and project timelines.

Are these final prices?

No. These are starting prices. The final cost depends on the number of pages, features, design complexity, content, deadline, and any additional requirements.

Do I need to pay a deposit?

Yes. A deposit is required before work begins. The deposit amount is agreed during the quotation stage and helps secure your project slot and begin the work.

How many revisions are included?

Most design and website packages include a reasonable number of revision rounds. Major changes outside the agreed project scope may require an additional quotation.

Can I request a custom package?

Yes. If your needs do not fit one of the listed packages, you can request a custom quotation based on the exact work you need.

How long does a project take?

Timelines depend on the project size and how quickly content, feedback, and approvals are provided. A clear timeline is included in the quotation before work starts.
